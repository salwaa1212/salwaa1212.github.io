    Description

    Background

    In recent years, there have been significant challenges related to the low awareness and spirit of patriotism among the younger generation, especially for domestic products. Globalization, urbanization, changes in social values, and various complex problems have influenced the younger generation's perceptions and priorities toward Indonesia.

    Goals

    Promoting local products among the younger generation of Indonesia, as a form of love for the country; Building a platform for the younger generation that provides a variety of local products; Creating experiences that motivate the younger generation to be involved in supporting the regional economy and culture as an embodiment of nationalism.

    User Target

    21-year-old man, born and raised in one of the metropolitan cities in Indonesia. Apart from that, he often buys foreign products because he considers domestic products lower quality.
